Safety Measures for Students.

Always be aware of your surroundings, report suspicious activities, and utilize campus security services when needed.

Take precautions when walking alone at night, stay in well-lit areas, and trust your instincts.

Carry a small personal safety device, such as a whistle or pepper spray, for added security.

Familiarize yourself with emergency contact numbers and campus security locations.

Frequently check reported incidents on the dashboard to stay informed about safety concerns in your area.
